  The Maison

A French house built for those who have seen the postcards — and want the place itself.

"All of our travels are curated by our team of Luxury Experts."

Founded in Paris in 2003, Safrans du Monde was born from a single conviction: that the great journeys of the world should be effortless. The maison designs private-jet expeditions and small-group grand-luxury tours in which every transfer, every table, every threshold has already been arranged — so the traveller is free to simply arrive.

Its signature is French savoir-faire applied to logistics: privileged access to sites after hours, legendary palace hotels held under exclusive contract, expert lecturers and local guides, and a concierge cadence that anticipates rather than reacts. Whether a honeymoon, a milestone, or the trip one has promised oneself for a lifetime, the house tailors it to the guest.
